Agent Bio

I was born and raised in this community. I grew up biking the Monon, serving pizza slices at Bazbeaux's, and representing North Central high school as both a diver and captain of the varsity softball team. After following my initial dream of pursuing a career in Sport Management I attended Flagler College in St. Augustine, Florida. I realized there's truly nothing like the Midwest and came back home. Initially I worked for Northwestern Mutual as a Financial Advisor, and while working in the corporate world provided me with great experience, it didn't give me the satisfaction I wanted out of a career. I wanted to wake up and be excited to go to work!

After some soul searching and talking things over with my family, I decided to partner with my mother in real estate and our partnership was formed. We're third and fourth generation realtors who not only bring a wealth of experience, but also a passion, to the process of buying and selling homes. I understand that buying a home is a huge deal. The process can seem daunting, but like any former athlete, I'm not intimidated by challenges; I'm inspired by them.

I'm passionate about the strong sense of community Indianapolis has to offer, and have loved watching my hometown grow into a nationally recognized city. I work to share that passion with buyers, helping them navigate the different neighborhoods and vibrant cultural districts have to offer. When I'm off the clock, if you can truly call that, you can always find me exploring my city with my high school sweetheart turned husband and our children Leah, Dottie, and James. It seems like every day there's something new going on in Indy, be it a restaurant opening, new microbrew debut, or a cultural festival. I'm an active person and a go-getter. I've heard people say there isn't much to do in Indy, I'm here to prove to those people that if you're bored, you're boring. Let me help you find all this great city has to offer and help you make it feel like home.
